Bricolet — Privacy Policy

Oura integration · Last updated 2026-06-06

Bricolet is a local-first personal data tool. When you connect your Oura account, Bricolet uses Oura’s official API, with your explicit authorization, to read only the following data:

  • Sleep sessions (timing, stages, average HRV and heart rate)
  • Daily readiness and activity summaries (steps, calories)
  • Recorded workouts (type, duration, intensity)

What stays local

All data retrieved from Oura is stored encrypted on your own device. It is never transmitted to a Bricolet server or any third party, and is never sold or shared.

What we never take

Your Oura account password (it stays with Oura), and raw continuous heart-rate or location data.

Read-only access

Bricolet’s access is read-only. You can revoke it at any time from your Oura account settings, and delete the locally stored data and tokens on your device.

Independence

This integration is independent and not endorsed by or affiliated with Oura Health Oy.
